Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish. Fried rice is a popular component of East Asian, Southeast Asian and certain South Asian cuisines, as well as a staple national dish of Indonesia.

A homemade dish, fried rice is typically made with ingredients left over from other dishes, leading to countless variations. Fried rice first developed during the Sui Dynasty in China and as such all fried rice dishes can trace their origins to Chinese fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice, cooked al dente
  • Carrots
  • Beans
  • Green Peas
  • Spring Onions
  • Panner
  • Ground Cumin
  • Ground Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Ground Star Anise

Steps to make tasty Fried Rice!!

  1. First, wash the rice and cook it al dente. Then keep it aside.
  2. Cut all the vegetables and keep them ready for sauteing.
  3. Cut panner into cubes and shallow fry them till they are light brown.
  4. Put some oil in the deep frying pan and let it heat.
  5. Put all the vegetables in the pan, and let them fry.
  6. Pour some soy sauce, and put all the spices.
  7. Mix the rice with the vegetables, and Ta-Da!! Your Fried Rice is ready!
