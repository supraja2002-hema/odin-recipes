Lemon Rice

Description

Lemon Rice also known as Chitranna or Nimmakaya Pulihora is a crunchy, flavorful, tasty and tangy dish that is easy to make and tastes so good. Lemon juice, fried nuts, aromatic herbs and spices combine perfectly to give this steamed rice an amazing spicy, tangy and nutty flavor. The nuts add a crunch for the perfect rice dish texture. This vegan lemon rice recipe is sure to become one of your favorite rice dishes.

Ingredients

  • Rice, fully cooked
  • Fried mustard seeds
  • Urad dal
  • Green Chillies
  • Herbs & Spices
  • Fried peanuts
  • Cashews
  • Lemon juice

Steps to make tasty Lemon Rice!!

  1. First, wash the rice, put turmeric in the rice and cook it completely. Then keep it aside.
  2. Squeeze the lemon juice in a bowl and keep it aside.
  3. Pour some oil in a pan, and fry the green chillies,peanuts and the spices till they become gold and crunchy.
  4. Set aside the peanuts and then add cashews in the same pan.
  5. Fry the cashews till they are golden and set aside.
  6. Take the rice in a big container and mix all the above ingredients.
  7. Ta-Da!! Your Lemon Rice is ready!
